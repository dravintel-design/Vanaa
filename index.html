Our Mission

VANA's mission is to make tree planting as easy and accessible as ordering a product online. In just minutes, anyone can choose and order trees through an AI-powered platform, and VANA will plant them locally in their area. Our goal is to become a revolutionary force in greening India — connecting technology, people, and nature to create a greener future.

How Vana Was Born

A classroom moment that
changed everything.

"

The idea for Vana was born from a moment in my classroom. During one of our lessons, my teacher was explaining the effects of global warming. An image was shown on the screen — a polar bear and its cub standing on a small piece of remaining ice, surrounded by water as the ice around them had melted.

That image stayed with me. It was more than just a picture; it showed the real impact of climate change and how global warming is affecting life on our planet.

That day, I made a decision: if I ever built a business, it should create a positive impact and contribute to reducing global warming. From that thought, Vana was born.

— Lokendra Lingam, Age 15, Founder & CEO

Vana is not just a tree plantation service company. It is a step towards restoring nature, creating greener spaces, and building a better future for the next generation. Every tree planted is a contribution towards a healthier planet.

Vana began with a vision — to grow trees today for a better tomorrow.
